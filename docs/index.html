What is JITAI Medi Bot?

JITAI Medi Bot delivers personalised medication reminders directly to your WhatsApp. It learns your schedule and preferred communication style, then sends gentle nudges at the right times — helping you build consistent habits without feeling overwhelmed.

How it works

⏰

Set your schedule

Tell the bot your morning and evening routine times during a short onboarding chat.

💬

Get reminders

Receive a personalised message at your chosen times, in a tone that works for you.

✅

Log check-ins

Reply Y or N to log whether you took your medication. That's all it takes.

🔕

Quiet hours

Set a do-not-disturb window and the bot will never send during those hours.

Your privacy

We store only what is needed to deliver reminders: your phone number, schedule preferences, and Y/N check-ins. We do not store medication names, diagnoses, or raw message content. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Important notice

JITAI Medi Bot is a reminder and habit-tracking tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. See our Terms of Service.
